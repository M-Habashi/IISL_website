2nd Midwest Smart Structures Colloquium

 

 

 

 

 

 

 
Welcome!
The Midwest Smart Structures Colloquium is an opportunity to share ideas and build friendships between the IISL and the SSTL. The two day event features various presentation sessions and group activities. We are excited to welcome you to West Lafayette!
Important Notes
Bowen Laboratory Address
1040 S. River Road
West Lafayette, IN, 47907
Dress Code & Lab Tour
The dress code for the conference sessions is business casual, and for all the out-of-conference activities is casual. We will have a lab tour on Friday evening at the end of the sessions. To comply with safety regulations, please bring pair of closed-toe shoes; hard-hats and safety goggles will be provided at Bowen Laboratory.
Family Events
Families are welcome and encouraged to attend our dinner on Friday, banquet on Saturday, and Feast of the Hunter's Moon on Sunday morning. Please refer to the links below (Optional Activities and Recreation Guide) for activities they can enjoy while we are at the colloquium.
Dinner
Dinner on Friday will be outdoors (time permitting). Feel free to bring outdoor games such as balls, frisbees, etc.
Banquet
There will be awards for the best presentations! The award party banquet will be at a bowling alley. This is a family-friendly establishment. Dinner will be provided and beer can be purchased at each individual's expense.
