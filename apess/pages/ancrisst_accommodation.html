ACCOMMODATIONS

Two housing options will be suggested, including one on-campus and one at a nearby hotel. These include:

First Street Towers, Purdue University Residences

Address: 1250 1st Street, West Lafayette, IN 47906

First Street Towers is conveniently located on the west side of the Purdue campus within easy walking distance of the conference venue (10 min walk) and the airport shuttle drop-off location (5 min walk). Single room accommodations are provided, including towels, linens, an in-suite bathroom, and University Wifi access. Parking will also be available at the McCutcheon Parking Garage for those choosing this accommodation option.

Rooms may be reserved through the Conference Registration page. The nightly rate for a single-occupancy room (taxes, fees and hot breakfast are included):

  • Accommodation Price: $83.00/night

https://www.housing.purdue.edu/Housing/Residences/FirstStreetTowers/index.html

Courtyard Marriott Lafayette

Address: 150 Fairington Avenue, Lafayette, IN 47905

Phone:765-449-4800

The Lafayette Courtyard Marriott is a 15 min drive from campus and is a drop-off location for the Reindeer and Lafayette Limo shuttle services from the Indianapolis airport (see Travel page). A shuttle from the hotel to campus will be made available throughout the conference for the convenience of the ANCRiSST 2020 attendees. Parking passes at the conference venue are also available and can be reserved through the Conference Registration page.

Guests at the Courtyard Marriott will have the option to reserve either a single room or a double room. The nightly rate for a single- or double-occupancy room (hot breakfast and shuttle to conference venue are included):

  • Accommodation Price: $129.00/night + taxes (12%)

Reservations can be made for the ANCRiSST 2020 room block:

  • Calling the front desk at the number listed above

  • Contacting Riley Morris via email (rmorris@courtyardlafayette.com) or phone (765.449.4800 ext. 4934)

https://www.marriott.com/hotels/travel/lafcy-courtyard-lafayette/
