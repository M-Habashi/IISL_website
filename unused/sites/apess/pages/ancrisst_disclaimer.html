DISCLAIMER NOTICE

  1. The ANCRiSST 2020 Organizing Committee reserves the right to cancel or postpone ANCRiSST 2020 or to change the schedule or the venue without prior notice, due to the act of God or on the occurrence of any event of force majeure.
  2. In the case of any such contingency happening before the opening day, the ANCRiSST 2020 Organizing Committee shall refund any registration fees it will have received before the contingency, less related costs.
  3. Any air fares, accommodation fees, and other expenses paid by a registered participant shall not be compensated by the ANCRiSST 2020 Organizing Committee but shall be subject to an agreement between the registered participant and businesses in charge.
  4. In such a contingency stated above in (1)
    - If it happens during the meeting: the ANCRiSST 2020 Organizing Committee shall not refund any registration fees.
    - If it happens before the opening causing the ANCRiSST 2020 Organizing Committee to be unable to hold ANCRiSST 2020, it shall refund registration fee in principle.
  5. When postponement, change in ANCRiSST 2020 duration and/or change of the venue cause applicants to be unable to participate in the conference, the ANCRiSST 2020 secretariat shall refund any registration fee only to such an applicant who desires a refund.
  6. No damages or compensation, except the refunding of registration fees as stated above in (2), shall be recovered from the ANCRiSST 2020 Organizing Committee.
