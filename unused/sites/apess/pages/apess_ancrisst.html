ANCRiSST 2020

APESS 2020 is organized under the auspices of the Asian-Pacific Network of Centers for Research in Smart Structures Technology (ANCRiSST), which was established in 2002. The 15th International Workshop (ANCRiSST 2020) will be held on the campus of the Purdue University on July 17-20, 2020.

The purpose of the workshop is to assess the current progress in smart materials and structures technologies and to develop synergies among researchers in various disciplines from different countries that will facilitate joint research projects that are of such scope and magnitude that cannot be easily carried out by the individual centers.
