REGISTRATION

Registration to APESS 2020 will consist of a 2-phase application and registration process. Only applicants who have been accepted will be asked to register. Application decisions will be returned on March 27, 2020.

Application

Application to APESS 2020 is now open! Please apply HERE.

As part of their application, students will have the opportunity to apply for a scholarships which will cover the majority of the cost of APESS registration and accommodation. Those attending APESS for the first time are eligible to apply for such Scholarships.

Decisions will be returned on March 27, 2020. Those accepted will be invited to register at that time.

No fees will be assessed during the application phase.

Registration

Registration for APESS 2020 will open on March 27, 2020.

Registration Fee: $500.00

Registration Costs Include:

  • Lectures
  • Laboratory Projects
  • APESS 2020 Welcome Reception
  • Technical and Cultural Events
  • Attendance to ANCRiSST 2020 Conference (17-20 July, 2020) included in fee

Registration costs will not include accommodation fees. More information on the costs of accommodation is included in the Accommodation Tab.

Additional information on registration and payment will be made available to accepted applicants on March 27, 2020. For any additional questions, please contact us at apess2020@purdue.edu.
